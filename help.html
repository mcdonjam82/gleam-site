What to Text Gleam

Every command you can send from your phone — no app, no login, no guesswork.

📱
All commands are texted to your Gleam number: +1 (888) 394-4345. Save it as Gleam Reviews so it's easy to find. Commands are not case-sensitive — APPROVE and approve both work.
⭐

Approving & Handling Reviews

APPROVE

Post the drafted response exactly as written. Gleam sends it live to Google immediately.

Shortcut: A

EDIT

Reply EDIT followed by your revised response text. Gleam posts your version instead of the draft.

Example: EDIT Thanks for the feedback — we'll make it right.

IGNORE

Skip this review and take no action. No response will be posted. The review stays on Google unanswered.

Shortcut: I

PENDING

Snooze this review for later. Gleam will remind you about it in 24 hours if you haven't acted on it.

Shortcut: P

🤖

Auto-Response Settings

Choose which reviews Gleam posts automatically — without texting you first. All others still come to you for approval.

Send one of these to change your auto-post rule
AUTO 5

Auto-post responses to 5-star reviews only. 1–4 star reviews come to you for approval.

AUTO 4+

Auto-post responses to 4 and 5-star reviews. 1–3 star reviews come to you for approval.

AUTO ALL

Auto-post responses to every review — Gleam handles everything. You won't be texted unless a review triggers a sentiment alert.

AUTO NONE

Send every review to you for approval before posting. Nothing goes live without your say-so.

Status & Navigation

STATUS

Get a quick summary of your current setup — auto-response rule, pending reviews, and your current average rating.

MENU

Receive a short list of all available commands by text. Useful if you forget a keyword while on the go.

🔔

Alerts & Preferences

SENTIMENT ON

Turn on sentiment alerts. Gleam texts you when multiple reviews mention the same issue — pricing, response time, workmanship — so you can spot patterns early.

SENTIMENT OFF

Turn off sentiment alerts. You'll still receive individual review notifications — just no pattern summaries.

REMINDERS ON

Turn on reminder texts for pending reviews. If you haven't acted on a review in 24 hours, Gleam will follow up once.

REMINDERS OFF

Turn off reminder texts. Pending reviews stay in your queue — Gleam just won't nudge you again.

💬
Command not working? Text MENU to get a list of valid commands sent back to your phone. For anything else, email us at hello@gleamreply.com and we'll sort it out same day.
